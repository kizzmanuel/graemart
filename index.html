Frequently Asked Questions

Some question concerning our service and product.

What is the concept of your service?

At our company, we offer a unique shopping experience. You can place an order with a list of items you need, and our team will purchase and deliver those items directly to your doorstep, on a one-off and scheduled basis. Third-party orders can also be placed and delivered as custom packages.

How do I place an order?

We are currently Live and operational in Abuja. While you can make a list and order from anywhere, our delivery network is limited to these specified service areas and we are dedicatedly working to be in your City soon, if not yet. Please check our website to verify if we deliver to your location..

Can I order from any location?

Placing an order is easy! Simply create a list of the items you want using our user-friendly online platform. You can also upload an existing shopping list if you have one, indicate your budget range, schedule plan and we will handle the rest for you.

How do I pay for my order?

We offer various secure payment options, including credit/debit cards, online payment gateways, and more. Your payment information is handled with the utmost confidentiality and security.

Are there any fees for your service?

Yes, there is a service fee associated with each order. This fee covers the shopping, packaging, and delivery processes, ensuring that you receive the highest quality service.
