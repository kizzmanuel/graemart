Delivery and Return Policy

Delivery Process:

At graemart , we are committed to providing a seamless and convenient shopping experience for our valued customers.

Our delivery process is designed to ensure your orders are processed efficiently and delivered to your doorstep in a timely manner. Here's how it works:

1. Placing an Order:- Indicate your purchase Budget,Browse our extensive range of products and list in your desired Product, Quantity and Variety to your shopping cart. Once you've finalized your selection, proceed to the checkout page.

2. Providing Delivery Details: - During the checkout process, you will be prompted to provide accurate delivery information, including your address, contact number, and any specific delivery instructions.

3. Order Confirmation: - After successfully placing your order, you will receive an order confirmation email that includes a summary of your purchased items and delivery details.

4. Processing and Packaging: - Our team will promptly process your order and ensure that your items are carefully packaged to prevent any damage during transit.

5. Dispatch and Tracking: - Once your package is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your delivery.

6. Delivery to Your Doorstep: - Our reliable delivery partners will handle the transportation of your package. You can expect your items to be delivered to your provided address within the estimated delivery timeframe.

Return and Refund Policy:

We understand that occasionally there might be a need to return an item. Our return and refund policy is designed to provide a hassle-free process for such situations:

1. Eligibility for Returns:Perishables are not eligible for returns once receipt has been confirmed.

- Items purchased from graemart can be returned within 24-4830 days from the date of delivery for non-perishables.

- To be eligible for a return, the item must be in its original condition, unused, and in the same packaging.

2. Initiating a Return:- To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team via email or phone. Provide your order number and the reason for the return.

3. Return Authorization:

- Once your return request is approved, you will receive detailed instructions on how to proceed, including the address to which you should send the item.

4. Return Shipping: - Customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping. We recommend using a reliable shipping method with tracking to ensure the safe return of the item.

5. Inspection and Refund:

- Upon receiving the returned item, our team will inspect it to ensure it meets the return eligibility criteria.

- Once the inspection is complete, we will process your refund. Please note that it may take a few business days for the refund to be reflected in your original payment method.
